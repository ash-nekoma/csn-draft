PVP ARENA MECHANICS & T.O.S.

Welcome to the High-Stakes PVP Arena. Before entering, you must agree to the following rules:

1. MATCH WAGERS & THE POT
Both duelists must agree on a wager amount. The server will deduct the wager from both players and create a locked Pot. The winner takes the Pot.

2. HOUSE RAKE (TAX)
To maintain the servers and provide secure escrow, the Casino deducts a strict 5% House Tax from the final Pot before paying out the winner.

3. SPECTATOR BETTING
Spectators can bet on Player 1 or Player 2 to win the entire match. Winning spectator bets pay 1.95x against the House. To prevent collusion, Spectator Betting is disabled for choice-based games like RPS and Tic-Tac-Toe. Max spectator bet is 50,000 TC.

4. DISCONNECTION RULES
If a seated duelist disconnects before the match actively begins, the Arena will Abort and all TC will be safely refunded. If a player disconnects during an active match round, it counts as a Technical Knockout (TKO), and the remaining player instantly wins the Pot.

5. VOICE CHAT
Hold the Spacebar (or click the Mic button) to transmit voice to the entire Arena. Maximum talk time is 10 seconds per press. Keep it clean. Toxicity may result in an Admin ban.
